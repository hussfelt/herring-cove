Read a blogpost regarding emails sent to developers about “The next thing/app/service”.

Found myself thinking: “I wonder how many emails like this I have responded to”, coming up with a number far greater than 100…

Then I thought of all the meetings I have been to before I learned to say:

No thank you.

I am always glad to help, and I will always try my best to guide people if I can. But a good advice is to learn to say no. It took me quite a while and a number of failures…
